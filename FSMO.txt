FSMO (Flexible single master operation) is the role of the AD
It has 5 different roles
forest master, domain master ,Relative ID , Primary Domain Controller ,Infrastructure master


schema master is the collection of classes and attributes

domain master is responsible for promote and demote domain controlers.Domain namig master is resposible to create add, and prevent duplicate name create in the forest.

Relative ID is responsible to assign unique identification to the objects

Primary Domain Controller is responsible to synchronize time accross the forest.

Infrastructure Master is responsible to update user A/C information when its moved to different domain.
